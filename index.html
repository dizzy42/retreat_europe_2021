Retreat Buddy Requirement for NEW Participants of SPIRITUAL RETREAT (Only)

All NEW Participants of SPIRITUAL RETREAT MUST be accompanied by an in-person "Retreat Buddy" who meets the minimum requirements below. There is no buddy requirement for Inner Heart or MRT workshops, only for Spiritual Retreat. Retreat Buddies must:

  • Have attended 3 x Spiritual Retreat
  • Have completed 10 x lifetime regressions that have been counted
  • One buddy per new participant. Only those buddies who have also attended True Self Retreat can be a buddy for 2 new participants maximum.

NOTE: We are not facilitating buddies for new participants while still in COVID lockdown. We encourage new participants to join only if you are able to find a buddy that you know and are comfortable with.

PREREQUISITES FOR NEW PARTICIPANTS

Please note that the following prerequisites must be fulfilled by all NEW Participants of each workshop.

 

Inner Heart Workshop
  • Have attended Reiki TUMMO Levels 1, 2, 3A, Meditation and Kundalini OR have attended Open Heart Workshops Level 1-6 OR OR have attended Secrets of Natural Healing Workshops Level 1-6
  • 5x Open Heart Prayer/Meditation with senior alumni or coordinators online or in person
Spiritual Retreat
  • Have attended Inner Heart Workshop
  • Must be accompanied and supported directly, in-person, in the same place by a Retreat Buddy who meets the requirements listed above
  • Must sign and upload “New SR Participant Statement” as part of registration
Master Yoga 1-3
  • Have practiced MY1-2 21x (max once/day)
  • 20x Open Heart Prayer/Meditation with senior alumni or coordinators online or in person after starting MY1-2 practice
Master Yoga 1-4
  • Have practiced MY1-3 21x (max once/day)
MRT
  • Have attended Inner Heart twice
  • Have attended Spiritual Retreat twice
  • Able to feel with the inner heart
  • Have done Open Heart Prayer a minimum of 15 times through online prayer sessions or in a group with senior alumni
